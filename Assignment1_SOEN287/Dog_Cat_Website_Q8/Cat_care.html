How to take care of your cat?

Your pet needs your care and attention. They need you for their many needs.

  1. Make sure you have plenty of human companionship.
  2. Maintain a steady supply of fresh water and regular, appropriate meals.
  3. It should be groomed on a regular basis. Longhaired cats require grooming on a daily basis.
  4. Vaccinate your cat on a regular basis against the major feline diseases.
  5. Take them for routine veterinary examinations.
  6. When the cat exhibits any signs of illness, take it to the veterinarian.

These are just few suggestions for your new cat. For more information you can visit icatcare
